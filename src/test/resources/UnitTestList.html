6. Settlers are moving on a complex route and it's last tile is occupied by other Settlers. They can't as the swapping must be the first movement
7. Two different type of units (Settlers, Civil and Warriors, Military) can be stacked on the same tile
8. Workers can enter into empty city
9. Workers can't enter into a city occupied by other civil unit (Great Artist)
10. All possible to move in tiles for Workers must not include mountains and foreign workers
11. Another map, all possible to move in tiles for Workers must not include mountains
